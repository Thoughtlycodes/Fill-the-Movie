THE MOVIE GAME

INSTRUCTION

1. Try guessing letters of the movie, by entering the letters one by one in the small gap given.

2. Press submit.

3. If the entered letter is not right or already present your life decrease by one.

4. If your guess is correct the letter shows up in corresponding place.

5. If all the blank are filled your score would rise up by one.

6. After which you can click next to get the next movie.

7. At the beginning of each game you would be awarded with 8 lifes.

8. When your last life finishes the game ends.
